About Anil's Food Zone

Discover the story behind our passion for authentic ramen and our commitment to bringing you the finest Japanese dining experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Chef Anil Kumar, Anil's Food Zone began as a small dream to bring authentic Japanese ramen culture to India. After spending 8 years training under master ramen chefs in Tokyo, Chef Anil returned with a mission to create the perfect bowl of ramen.

What started as a humble 12-seat restaurant has grown into a beloved culinary destination, but our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged. Every bowl is still prepared with the same attention to detail and passion that Chef Anil learned in the bustling ramen shops of Shibuya.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of ramen enthusiasts while maintaining our core values: fresh ingredients, traditional techniques, and warm hospitality that makes every visit feel like coming home.

Our Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional Japanese ramen-making techniques while respecting local tastes and preferences.

Quality

Only the finest, freshest ingredients make it into our bowls. We source locally when possible and import specialty items from Japan.

Community

We're more than a restaurant - we're a gathering place for food lovers, families, and friends to create lasting memories.
